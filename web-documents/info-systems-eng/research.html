Research

As an interdisciplinary fundamental research & R&D learning institute, CIISE houses state-of-the-art research in innovative applications of information systems.

Researchers Research Labs

Research Laboratories

CIISE has three affiliated research laboratories. Our research is powered by cutting-edge equipment and computing facilities.

Grants & Funding

CIISE has received over 3.4 million research funding from various sources, including NSERC, DND, NATEQ, Infrastructure Canada, MRST, PROMPT-Québec, and Concordia University.
